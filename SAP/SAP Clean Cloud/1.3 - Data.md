### Three Types of Data in SAP S/4HANA

| **Type**          | **Description**                                                                                                                                                                                          | **Examples**                                  |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **Configuration** | Defines the organization's structure                                                                                                                                                                     | Company codes, plants, purchase organizations |
| **Master**        | Identifies the enterprise's core entities                                                                                                                                                                | Customers, vendors, products, G/L accounts    |
| **Transactional** | Derived from transactions directly<br><br>Always involves:<br>- **Time value** (ex. date)<br>- **Numerical value** (ex. amount)<br>- Reference to at least one **master object** (ex. customer involved) | Sales orders, purchase orders, hiring         |
### Characteristics of data quality
- **Accuracy**
- **Completeness**
- **Consistency**
- **Timeliness**
- **Validity** (data should confirm to defined rules)
- **Uniqueness** (i.e. minimize duplicate data)

### Data Volume
- **Data volume** is also important as useless data clogs and clutters the system
