### Steps in Delivery
1. **Outbound delivery** - initiated as the basic document for shipping
	- Elements include:
		- Picking goods
		- transportation
		- goods issue
	1. Referenced to at least one sales order
		1. Ship-to-party (client)
		2. material
		3. quantity
		4. are all copied
2. **Goods issue** - the shipping specialist posts a good issue. This confirms the delivery and reduces the stock, creating the...
3. **Material document**
	- **Stocks and requirements & inventory management**
		- Inventory & MPR are reduced by the delivered quantity
	- **Material ledger**
		- Balance sheet is updated
	- **Accounting & controlling document** - docu for controlling is generated
	- **Billing due list** - delivery is made available for billing
	- **Document flow** - the status in all associated documents are updated
### Elements of a Delivery Document
- **Header** - ship-to-party, deadlines
- **Item** - material, quantity, stock information 
### Other Terms
- **Picking** - the process of retrieving items from inventory to prepare them for shipping
