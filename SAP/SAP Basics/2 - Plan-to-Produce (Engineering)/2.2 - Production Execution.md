- **Production order** - defines which materials are to be produced in which quantity, time, and way
- **Planned orders** are to be converted to **production orders**
- Manufacturing status is sent either by a production operator (manual assembly) or an assembly robot (automated)

### Steps of Production Order
1. **Order creation**
	- Master data is required, including routing and schedules
2. **Order release**
	- Making the order available to production
	- Goods issues, confirmations, and goods receipts can be posted during production
3. **Goods issues**
	- After issuing, stock quantities and stock values of components are decreased
4. **Confirmation**
	- Done for each of the various steps of the order production
5. **Goods receipt**
	- Goods receipt is uploaded to S/4HANA
	- Stock quantities and stock values of manufactured goods are increased

### In S/4 HANA...
- You can convert a planned order into a production order
- You can check production order status, and OK them if needed depending on the individual material
- You can also confirm production processes, thereby finishing them(?) and increasing stock
