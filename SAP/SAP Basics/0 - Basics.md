### SAP
- A business suite of applications that connect multiple departments of a company
- "The brain of a company"
- Tracking sales, managing finances, keeping inventory, etc. streamlined into one system
- Crucial aspects:
	- Adaptability
	- Seamless integration
	- Real-time
	"Flexible rigidity"
### Parts of an ERP System
- Cloud ERP - The core of the system
- Digital ERP Core - Contains API, data integrations, etc.
- Extension Capabilities - Core HR, SCM, etc. functions
- Business-Specific Systems - other systems related to the business
