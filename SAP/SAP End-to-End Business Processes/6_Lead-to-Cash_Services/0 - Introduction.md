# Services of SAP

There is both Service Management and **Sales & Order Management** (different from S&OP[^S&OP}]) in SAP.

Service Management can further be divided into:
- **Customer Service** (SAP Service Cloud)
- **Field Service** (SAP Field Service Management)

[^S&OP]: Sales & Operations Planning
