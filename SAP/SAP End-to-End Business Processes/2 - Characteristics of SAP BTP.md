> **NOTE!** BTP = Business Technology Platform
### SAP BTP
- **SAP BTP** - The part of SAP responsible for *integrations* and *extensions*
	- i.e. all the specific application stuff that differentiate a business
	- This is where, presumably, coding efforts usually go towards
- **SAP Business Accelerator Hub** - free integration packages, extension templates
