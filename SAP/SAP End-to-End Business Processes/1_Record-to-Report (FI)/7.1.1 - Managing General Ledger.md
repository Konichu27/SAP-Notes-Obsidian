## General Journal Entry
- Represents a *single* transaction
- Contains a **document header** (document-general info) and **document items** (transaction-specific info)

### Elements of a General Journal Entry
- **Journal Entry Date** - date of issue of original document
- **Posting Date** - date of entry in system
- **Journal Entry Type** - includes Vendor Invoice (KR), Payment Posting (ZP), Asset Posting (AA)
- **Company Code** - the level of journal entries
- **General Ledger Account** - must be associated with the operating CoA of the company code
- **Debit**, **Credit** - updated accordingly
- **Total Balance** - *credit and debit must be equal!!!!!*
	- (basic concept in accounting, particularly double-entry bookkeeping)

## Miscellaneous Info

> [!NOTE] G/L Updates
> General ledger accounts are *automatically updated* / created during activity in other business processes, for example when a goods issue is posted. 
> 
> Other times, however, it must be *inputted manually*---for example, when a donation is made towards the business .
