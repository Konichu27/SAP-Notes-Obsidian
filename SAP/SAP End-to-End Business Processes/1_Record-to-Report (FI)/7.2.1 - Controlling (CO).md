## Types of Cost Controlling
- **Overhead** - facility management
	- Cost center - Canteen -> causing cost centers (finance, sales consulting)
	- Product cost controlling also involves overhead
- **Work breakdown structure (WBS)** - Process hierarchy, and therefore costs associated with a single project (ex. a trade fair needs marketing brochures)
	 - Activity allocation - WBS element collects project costs
	 - Each WBS element is a **cost element** that functions as a cost center for all costs of a specific type.

Overall, the goal is to **set the balance to 0** (credit & debit). Any extra costs not predicted are usually tossed into margin analysis to guarantee this.

- Divided into the following:

- The basis of all hierarchies within the enterprise structure
