Recall: [[1.5 - Management Accounting]]

All of the following are based in the **Profit and Loss Accounts**, particularly in **General Ledger Accounting**:

* **Cost element accounting**
	* **Primary costs** - primary expense & revenue. Is *external* in nature
	* **Secondary costs** - cost flows from based on controlling, ex. facility management. Is internal in nature; i.e., money is only moving to and from the company
	* **Product cost controlling** - management of cost objects, ex. sales or production orders
		* **Product cost planning** - this is where *planned costs* are made
		* **Cost object controlling** - this is where actual costs are mad
		* **Variance analysis** - analyzes differences in costs
* **Overhead cost controlling** - *indirect* costs assigned to a cost object
	* Overhead is not directly assigned to a product first. It is allocated first into cost centers instead, which are then allocated into products.
	* **Cost center** - gathers costs of a specific type (ex. rent, marketing, admin)
* **Margin analysis** - analysis of **market segments**
