Recall: [[1.4.1 - Financial & Management Accounting]]

| **Accounting Type**               | **Description**                                                                                         | **Example entry**                                                                                          | **which updates...**                                                                  |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **G/L accounting**                | used as a reference for other ledgers                                                                   | Billing document in sales process                                                                          | Increase in balance sheet receivables, revenue posting                                |
| **Accounts payable subledger**    | the debts *from* the business (credit, liabilities). Usually affects the liabilities in the G/L account | Supplier account posting                                                                                   | Increase in liabilities G/L account, balance sheet                                    |
| **Accounts receivable subledger** | the debts *to* the business (debit, assets). Usually includes transactions from customer accounts       | Customer incoming payment                                                                                  | Reduction in customer account receivables, total receivables in G/L and balance sheet |
| **Asset accounting subledger**    |                                                                                                         | Asset acquisitons, transfers, retirement postings; more specifically, the individual transactions involved | Individual asset account, G/L account                                                 |
The gist is: multiple ledgers are updated and *you will have to memorize which updates what*.
