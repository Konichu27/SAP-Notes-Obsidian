## Recruit-to-Retire (HCM)
- Managed by **SAP SuccessFactors**
- Includes the following solutions:
	- **Onboarding**
		- All paperwork for onboarding is consolidated into a single platform
	- **Learning** - SuccessFactors has an LMS for employees
	- **Succession** - talent retention & succession
	- **Compensation**
	- **Performance & Goals**
	- **Career Development** - SAP SuccessFactors Career Development Plan (CDP)
	- **People Analytics**
	- **Employee Central**
		- Centralized information about each employee
	- **Recruiting** - Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
		- Job postings to multiple sites can be automated
