## Employee Central
- SAP SuccessFactor's **human resource information system** (**HRIS**)
- Centralized portal for employee data
### People Profile
- Employee profile in Employee Central
## Core Structure
- The unified system of records in Employee Central
![[Pasted image 20260222160803.png]]
1. **HR workflows** - processes, events, business rules
	- *Processes/Workflows* - approval process
	- *Events/Event reasons* - affects employment status
	- *Business rules* - application logic for transactions
2. **Self-service capabilities** - the interactions managers & employees have with the system
3. **Role-based permissions** - access control
4. **HR data** - person & employment details
	- *Employer personal data* - DoB, national ID, etc.
	- *Employment data* - Job title, compensation, etc.
 5. **Foundation objects** - organization, job, payment
