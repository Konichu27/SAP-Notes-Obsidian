- Official name: **SAP SuccessFactors Recruitment**
## Components of SAP SuccessFactors Recruitment
- **Job Requisition and Application Management** - creation of job requisitions, acquisitions, and sourcing
- **Self Service Career Site** - under *Career Site Builder*
- **Sourcing Analytics** - Check spend on recruitment activities; under *Recruiting Advanced Analytics*
- **Candidate Relationship Management** - deals with passive candidates, talent pools, and e-mail campaigns for <u>up to 250,000</u> candidates at once
## Recruiting Process
- Involves the **job requisition** form and how it's posted

1. What role needs to be fulfilled?
	- The job requisition form is defined with the following details:
		- Job title, job description, location, hiring manager, compensation data, other requirement details
	- *Route map* - approval path for a new requisition
		- w/i after a requisition posting, another user (like a senior HR manager) may review the posting
2. Where will the job be posted and advertised?
	- *Recruiting posting* - wherein career sites, recruiting agencies, and job boards are defined
	- *Private posting* - job postings are sent to candidates discreetly
3. How will candidate data be collected?
	- *Candidate profile* - includes resume parsing and LinkedIn integration
	- *Application*
	- *Pre-screening questions*
4. What happens after the candidate applies?
	- *Talent pipeline* - the hiring process of the organization
	- *Candidate workbench*
	- Interview scheduling, interview assessments, background check, etc. are also available
5. What is the job offer process?
	- *Offer letter* - email or career site options are available. Additionally, it can be accepted "verbally"
