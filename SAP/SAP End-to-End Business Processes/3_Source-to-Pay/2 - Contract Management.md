| **Outline Agreement**    | **Document Volume**                                                                                                                                       | **Automatic Materials Planning**                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Contract**             | **New PO is required** with every release of goods or services not in the contract                                                                        | Requirements planning can be set up so that the contract item is automatically assigned to a requisition item as the source of supply.<br><br>However, the requisition must subsequently be converted in a purchase order (contract release order). |
