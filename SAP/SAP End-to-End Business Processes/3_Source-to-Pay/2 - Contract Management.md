- The stage wherein supplier contracts are settled and quotes are processed

## Outline Purchasing Agreements
- The **long-term agreement** between supplier and vendor, on the provision of goods
- Does not focus on specific delivery dates or delivery quantities. It just lists down the contract
	- Exception is when there's a **scheduling agreement**, in which case shipping dates are automatically booked

### Types of Outline Agreements
| **Outline Agreement**    | **Documents Needed**                                                                                       | **Automatic Materials Planning**                                                                                                                                       |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Contract**             | **New PO** for every goods/services not in the contract                                                    | Requisitions must be **manually** converted into POs.<br><br>However, the contract item can automatically be assigned to the requisition item as the source of supply. |
| **Scheduling Agreement** | **Delivery schedule line** that defines the date of delivery of goods.<br><br>It is extended continuously. | Set-and-forget (in the planning run)                                                                                                                                   |
