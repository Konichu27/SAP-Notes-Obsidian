## Warehouse Management System
- Software solution that allows the management of a warehouse or distribution center
- In SAP, you can:
	- **Track the amount** of goods/materials
	- **Track the location** of every storage bin for a good/material
	- **Control and record** all movements of each good/material
	- **Control the putaway**[^putaway]
	- **Control the picking**[^picking]

[^putaway]: The storage of goods received from a supplier into a warehouse. I.e., you "put away" the item you just received for storage
[^picking]: The preparation to deliver a good/material from the warehouse to its destination (customer/plant). I.e., you "pick" the item from the warehouse
## SAP Extended Warehouse Management (EWM)
- SAP solution for complex warehouses that require multiple elements:
	- Serial number
	- Batch number
	- Minimum shelf life
	- Vendor-managed-inventory (VMI)
	- Yard Management
	- Value-added service (VAS)
