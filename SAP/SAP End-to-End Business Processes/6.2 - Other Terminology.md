- **Point-to-point connection** - a connection between two disparate systems, usually SAP, that share master data.
	- Ex. S/4 HANA & Ariba
	- *NOTE!* <u>All systems must have a direct connection with each other.</u> So for example you have 4 systems, you need 6 connections in this pattern.

- **Master data integration** - refers to syncing master data between all systems
- **Master data governance** - the management of master data. Involves consolidation & data quality management
