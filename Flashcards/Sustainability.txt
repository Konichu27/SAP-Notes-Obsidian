"What are the 4 key categories of SAP Sustainability solutions?","1. Sustainability & ESG Reporting<br>2. Climate Action<br>3. Circular Economy<br>4. Social Responsibility"
"What is the primary solution for **Sustainability & ESG Reporting**?","**SAP Sustainability Control Tower**"
"What is the business outcome of **SAP Sustainability Control Tower**?","Define organization-specific targets and track performance using ESG data management and insight tools."
"Which SAP solutions address **Climate Action**?","SAP Sustainability Footprint Management and SAP Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) Management."
"What is the key outcome of **Climate Action** solutions?","Reduce the carbon footprint across the entire value chain using actual business transaction data."
"Which SAP solutions are used for the **Circular Economy**?","SAP Responsible Design and Production and SAP Green Token."
"What is the business outcome of **Circular Economy** solutions?","Build circular supply chains and minimize waste by finding ways to reuse business outputs."
"Which solutions fall under **Social Responsibility**?","SAP Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) Management and SAP S/4HANA for product compliance."
"What is the goal of **Social Responsibility** solutions?","Analyze and manage the business impact on people and society within a broader global context."
"**SAP Sustainability Footprint Management** belongs to which category?","**Climate Action**"
"**SAP Green Token** and **Responsible Design and Production** belong to which category?","**Circular Economy**"
"Which solution is used for both **Climate Action** and **Social Responsibility**?","SAP Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) Management"
