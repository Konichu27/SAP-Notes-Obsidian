Example: LGMG
- Problems include an outdated e-commerce system, multilingual and multicurrency support, and redundancy in data
- SAP Commerce Cloud and SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition allowed for the following:
	- Integration between the two systems
	- Faster, streamlined order management
	- Customization & configurability
	- Automation, intelligent documentation, resulting in improvement in aftersales services
	- Unified master data hierarchy with accounting categories, models, rules
	- SAP Services and Support provided a "professional, technical team"; i.e. a personalized team
Example: Vileda
- Migrating from SAP Customer Relationship Management to cloud services allowed for a ticket system
- "Perfect Store" - allowed teams to be assigned to check the status of products in stores
- SAP Sales Cloud allows for quotes sent directly to business customers, instead of Excel spreadsheets
- SAP Customer Cloud recorded customers in online shops, and integration with SAP ERP allowed for faster order processing and transfer of inventory and order information
- SAP Service Cloud allows for social media integration
Example: Brussels Airport (yes they do airports too)
- They wanted to replace their outdated ERP system, which was too customized to the point of inflexibility
- SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition allowed for the following:
	- SAP Ariba - procure-to-pay processes
	- SAP Concur - expense management
	- SAP SuccessFactors - HR
	- SAP Analytics Cloud - reporting & planning
	- SAP Integration Suite - cloud integration
	- SAP Enable Now - e-learning, training
- User-friendliness allowed for easy purchase requisitions & orders on any device
- System is standardized toward all users
- Integration allows for automation of expense logs or purchase order approvals

Example: Blue Diamond Growers
- Before, the company operated in a variety of manual processes and documents that were time-intensive and error-prone (from scenario planning to logistics to obtaining order status)
	- US$300 million annually in SCM
- COVID-19 worsened things by extending logistics time
- The following were implemented:
	- SAP Signavio (business process analysis)
	- SAP Transport Management
	- SAP Business Network for Logistics
- The company plans to summarize thousands of delivery notes with genAI
