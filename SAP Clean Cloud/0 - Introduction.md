**SAP ERP** traditionally was a local software, however since the introduction of the cloud **SAP Cloud ERP** has been introduced
- Entirely cloud-native
- Allows for continuous updates

### Clean Core
- An "ideal" cloud ERP system
	- More precisely, *a system that is as close to standard as possible*
		- Standardized guidelines, best practices
	- Latest updates, integrations, cloud-compliant extensions
	- Optimal data quality & process design
	- No unnecessary features, customizations, and complexities
	- Minimizes technical debt and maximizes the transformation possible in SAP
	- Can involve not just new system creation but also conversion
- In SAP Cloud ERP, all systems are implied to have a "clean core"

### Challenges That Make Clean Core Necessary

#### Business Adaptability
- **Disruption of supply chains** - COVID-19, geopolitical tensions, natural disasters
	- Businesses must diversity supply sources, strengthen relationships with suppliers, and perform risk management
	- Digital integration & data analysis help this out
- **Customer preferences** - Changing trends, generations, and social media
	- Businesses must perform customer analytics, market research, and personalized experiences
	- "Customer-focused"
- **Regulatory compliance** - Standards in government & industry always change
	- Businesses must regularly monitor changes, compliance, as well as invest in training & education of current & future employees
	- Be constantly up-to-date and agile with the latest changes
#### Technological Change
- **New technological advancements** - AI and ML, cloud computing and big data, automation
	- Does not just benefit the business but also the customer and their satisfaction
- **Accelerating innovation** - competitiveness and adaptation
- **Optimizing business processes** - automation, streamlining
	- Includes everything from customer service & sales processes to SCM & HR
- **Fostering agility** - as innovation is to ideas, agility is to speed
#### Landscape Challenges
- **Business model adaptations** - businesses prioritize simplicity & flexibility because of changing demands
	- Entire new markets can suddenly appear without warning!
		- Being "first mover" is a priority for many businesses
- **Key application adjustments** - businesses may suddenly need to scale up or down infrastructure, integrate new technologies, or update and improve apps and websites
	- This is why cloud infrastructure is important! It offers advantages of scalability as according to business needs
- **Changing customer needs**

> [!NOTE] **In SAP Cloud ERP...**
> - Deployment is fast and adoption is easy, so *adaptability* and *tech change* is quick
> - There is a framework for building extensions, so businesses can move "on the fly" as changes occurr
