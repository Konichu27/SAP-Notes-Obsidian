## Recruit to Retire
- There are many factors in a company's workforce: hiring process, training, performance evaluation, salaries, health and welfare, etc.
- SAP aims to cover the full employee life-cycle; hence, "Recruit to Retire"
## Lead to Cash
- Order fulfillment
- After-sales services
- Accounts receivable management
- Payment collection
- Can be customized based on industry requirements, customer types, sales channels
## Source to Pay
- SAP uses analytics and ML to determine the best suppliers (materials or services)
- S/4HANA Cloud, SAP Ariba, SAP Fieldglass, SAP Concur, SAP Business Network
